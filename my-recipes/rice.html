How to boil rice

Intro

Learn how to cook rice perfectly every time! Below, I'm sharing my go-to method for how to cook white rice on the stove. It's easy to make in under 20 minutes, and it always yields tender, fluffy rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 part rice*
  • 1 and 1/2 to 2 parts water*
  • A little bit of olive oil

*volumetric measurement

Steps

  1. Wash the rice!
  2. Boil the water and oil
  3. Add rice, set heat to low and cover the pot
  4. Simmer for 15 minutes
  5. Remove the pot from heat and rest for a few minutes
